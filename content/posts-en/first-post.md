## Issue Analysis

### 1. Federal Reserve Maintains Interest Rates

The Federal Reserve decided to hold interest rates steady at current levels. While this aligned with market expectations, forward guidance suggested potential rate increases may be on the table if inflation re-accelerates.

**Implications**:
- Maintains economic growth momentum
- Continued vigilance on inflationary pressures
- Increased probability of future rate hikes

### 2. Big Tech Strength Continues

Technology stocks, particularly AI-related companies, led the market higher:
- **NVIDIA**: Upgraded earnings guidance on strong AI chip demand
- **Microsoft**: Cloud computing business showing robust growth
- **Apple**: Positive sentiment ahead of new product launches

### 3. Korea Connection

Korean semiconductor manufacturers are positioned to benefit from U.S. tech strength. Currency movements remain a key monitoring point, with won/dollar trading in the 1,200-1,210 range.
